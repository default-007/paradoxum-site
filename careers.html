§ Careers

Teach here. Shape the future of education in Siaya

The Paradox Academy seeks talented, passionate professionals who share our commitment to excellence in education. Whether you are a qualified educator, administrator, or support specialist, we invite you to consider joining us.

§ 01 Why join us

A community of educators, not just employees.

01

Purposeful environment

A supportive, collaborative working environment where colleagues share a deep commitment to the children in their care.

02

Professional development

Ongoing CBC training, pedagogical workshops, and exposure to current best practice. We invest in our teachers' growth.

03

Shape the future

An opportunity to shape the future of education in Siaya — to build something that will outlast the day-to-day.

04

Competitive remuneration

Fair compensation and a close-knit professional community where good work is recognised and celebrated.

05

Career growth

The Academy is expanding to include the GCSE pathway. As we grow, our team grows — new roles, new responsibilities, new horizons.

06

A school that listens

An open-door culture where ideas from any quarter — classroom, kitchen, grounds — are heard, considered, and acted on.

§ 03 · How to apply

Send a thoughtful application — we read every one.

If you would like to be considered for a current or future role, send your CV and a cover letter explaining what draws you to The Paradox Academy and how you'd contribute.

Use the subject line "Career Enquiry — [Your Discipline]" and address your message to the Administrator.

We respond to all serious applications, whether or not we have an open role. If a position arises that matches your background, we'll reach out.
