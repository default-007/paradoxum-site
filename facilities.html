§ Facilities

Spaces built to teach. Every room, every field, by design

The Paradox Academy campus is purposefully designed to provide a safe, stimulating, and modern environment for learning and growth. Every space supports our mission to develop the whole person.

§ 01 Around campus

Eight spaces, one campus.

01

Bright, modern classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated, and naturally lit classrooms create an optimal learning environment. Each room is equipped with age-appropriate resources, display areas celebrating learner work, and furniture suited to each level from Pre-Primary through Junior Secondary.

02

Science laboratory

Our fully equipped science laboratory enables hands-on experimentation and inquiry-based learning across biology, chemistry, and physics. Junior Secondary learners conduct practical investigations that deepen understanding and cultivate scientific rigour.

03

Computer & ICT lab

Our modern ICT lab provides learners with access to current hardware and software, supporting digital literacy from Lower Primary and enabling Computer Science coursework for JSS learners. Online safety and digital citizenship are taught from day one.

04

Library & resource centre

The intellectual heart of the campus — home to fiction, non-fiction, reference texts, periodicals, and digital resources. It nurtures a lifelong love of reading and supports independent study at every level.

05

Sports grounds & play areas

Our grounds include a football pitch, netball and volleyball courts, an athletics track, and age-appropriate play areas. Regular physical education and inter-school competitions promote fitness, teamwork, and school spirit.

06

Dining hall

A clean, spacious dining hall seats the entire school community for balanced, freshly prepared meals. Meal times are valued social occasions that build community and shared identity.

07

Assembly & multi-purpose hall

Our assembly hall hosts daily gatherings, performances, examinations, and celebrations. It is the central hearth of the Paradox community — where every term begins and every milestone is honoured.

08

Health & safety

Learner safety is our highest priority. The campus is fully fenced with controlled access. We maintain a sick bay with first aid provisions and qualified staff on duty. Emergency procedures and drills are conducted regularly.
