§ Admissions

Join the family. Applications open · Playgroup through Grade 9

We are honoured by your interest in The Paradox Academy. We welcome applications from families who share our devotion to excellence, integrity, and community — and we look forward to meeting you.

§ 01 The journey · How to apply

Five steps from enquiry to enrolment.

I

Inquire

Complete our online enquiry form or contact the Admissions Office directly to express interest and ask any questions you have about the school, the programme, or the application process.

Channels
Form · Phone · Email
II

Visit

Book a campus tour to experience the Paradox environment first-hand. Meet our staff, see our facilities, and feel the spirit of our community. Visits are arranged at a time that suits your family.

Duration
~60 minutes
III

Apply

Submit a completed Application Form together with the required documents and the non-refundable application fee. Our office will confirm receipt and outline the next steps.

Submit to
Admissions Office
IV

Assessment

Your child may be invited for an age-appropriate readiness assessment. This is not a pass/fail examination — it helps us understand how best to support your child's learning journey from day one.

Format
Age-appropriate
V

Offer

Successful applicants receive a formal offer letter, accompanied by a welcome pack, fee details, uniform list, and onboarding information. Acceptance secures a place for the coming academic year.

Welcome
to Paradox
§ 02 Levels of entry

Choose the right starting point.

We accept new applicants at every level, from the very first year of pre-primary through Junior Secondary. Transfer students are welcome at any level, subject to availability of places and assessment.

If you are uncertain about the appropriate level for your child, our admissions team will guide you through the options based on age, prior education, and readiness.

Explore the stages →

  • Pre-Primary 1 (PP1)Age 4+

  • Grade 4 — 6 · Upper PrimaryAges 9–12

§ 04 Fees, payment & bursaries

Investment in a distinguished education.

Our fee structure is competitive and reflective of the quality of education, facilities, and pastoral care we provide. Detailed fee schedules for each level — Pre-Primary, Primary, and Junior Secondary — are available from the Admissions Office upon request or following a campus visit. Flexible payment plans are available for families who need them.
