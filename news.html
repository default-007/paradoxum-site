§ 01 Latest stories

From around the Academy.

Events October 2025

Sports Day 2025 — a celebration of grit, grace, and team spirit

The annual Sports Day brought the whole community together for a day of athletics, inter-house competition, and family picnics under the Siaya sun.

Academic September 2025

JSS scholars showcase invention at the inaugural science fair

Grade 7 and 8 learners presented experiments, prototypes, and research projects to a packed hall of parents and visiting judges from across the region.

Community August 2025

Cultural Day — a vibrant celebration of heritage and identity

Music, drama, traditional dance, and stories of home filled the assembly hall. A reminder that excellence and rootedness travel together at Paradox.

Achievement July 2025

Debate team takes top honours at regional finals

The Paradox debating society competed against schools from across western Kenya, returning home with the regional shield and three individual awards.

Events June 2025

Prize-Giving Day honours the year's outstanding scholars

The signature event of the Paradox calendar — recognising academic, creative, sporting, and character achievement across every level of the school.

Announcements May 2025

Open Day welcomes prospective families to the campus

An afternoon of campus tours, classroom visits, and conversations with our leadership and teaching staff. Applications for the next academic year are open.
